Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans anytime?

+

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we automatically prorate any billing differences. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll only pay the difference for the remaining days. Downgrades will credit your account for the unused portion.

Is there a free trial?

+

All paid plans include a full 14-day free trial with access to all features. No credit card is required to start your trial. The Free plan remains completely free forever with no trial period or hidden limitations. You can explore all features risk-free before committing to a paid plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also support PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are securely processed through our PCI-compliant payment gateway with 256-bit encryption.

Can I cancel my subscription?

+

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. After cancellation, you'll maintain access to all paid features until the end of your current billing period. We never lock you into long-term contracts, and you can re-subscribe anytime without losing your data.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

+

Yes, we provide a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations and educational institutions. To qualify, simply contact our support team with your valid non-profit registration number or educational credentials. We also offer special pricing for startups and enterprise customers with custom needs.

Is my financial data secure?

+

Security is our top priority. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. Our platform undergoes regular security audits, complies with GDPR and CCPA regulations, and is hosted on SOC 2 certified infrastructure. We never store your banking credentials and use read-only access for account aggregation.

Still have questions? Contact our team - we're happy to help!
